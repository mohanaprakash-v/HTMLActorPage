Following the action-romance flick Thirumalai in 2003, Ghilli, a film directed by S. Dharani and produced by A. M. Rathnam, was released. It co-starred Trisha and Prakash Raj with Vijay as the lead.[38] Ghilli was the first Tamil film of all time to gross over ₹500 million, or ₹50 crore, at the domestic box office.
